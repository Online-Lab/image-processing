IT'S WONDERFUL !

But how does it work?

Copy the path

Choose a picture, copy it's path
and put it in your code.

Set the size

Set the preferred size in percentage or exact units and add into your code.

Get the image

Save your time and enjoy the result on your web page.

Example: percentage resize.
your code: <img src="../img/wildcat.jpg">
your picture source: http://animalplanet.com/wildcat.jpg
modified code: <img src="https://thumber.io/get/percent/A/http://animalplanet.com/wildcat.jpg">

* where "A" - percentage of the source image

Example: size in pixels.
your code: <img src="../img/wildcat.jpg">
your picture source: http://animalplanet.com/wildcat.jpg
modified code: <img src="https://thumber.io/get/200x200/http://animalplanet.com/wildcat.jpg">

* where "200x200" - size in pixels
